Air Fryer Sesame Chicken Thighs

These air fryer sesame chicken thighs are a delicious and easy-to-make dish that's perfect for a quick weeknight meal. The chicken is marinated in a savory sauce and then cooked to perfection in the air fryer, resulting in juicy and flavorful thighs with a crispy exterior.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Mix well.
  2. Add the chicken thighs to the marinade and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or up to overnight.
  3. Preheat the air fryer to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Remove the chicken from the marinade and discard the remaining sauce.
  5. Place the chicken in the air fryer and cook for 10-12 minutes, or until cooked through and nicely browned.
  6. Sprinkle the sesame seeds over the chicken before serving. Enjoy!

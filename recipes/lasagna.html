Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese. It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The combination of flavors and textures makes lasagna a favorite among many people around the world. Whether you prefer a traditional meat lasagna or a vegetarian version, this dish is sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 500g of minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 400g of canned tomatoes
  • 200g of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 250g of lasagna sheets
  • 500ml of béchamel sauce
  • 200g of shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the minced beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato sauce, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a separate pot, cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Then add a layer of lasagna sheets, followed by a layer of béchamel sauce and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of béchamel sauce and cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

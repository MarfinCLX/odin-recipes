German Chocolate Dump Cake

German Chocolate Dump Cake is a delicious and easy-to-make dessert that combines the rich flavors of chocolate, coconut, and pecans. This cake is called a "dump cake" because you simply dump all the ingredients into a baking dish and bake it until it's golden and bubbly. The result is a moist and decadent cake with a gooey coconut-pecan topping that is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you're looking for a quick weeknight dessert or a crowd-pleasing treat for a special occasion, German Chocolate Dump Cake is the perfect choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of German chocolate cake mix
  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup of shredded coconut
  • 1 cup of chopped pecans
  • 1/2 cup of butter, melted

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix and condensed milk. Mix until well combined.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a greased baking dish.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the coconut, pecans, and melted butter.
  5. Sprinkle the coconut-pecan mixture over the cake batter.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
